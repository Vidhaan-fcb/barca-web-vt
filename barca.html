About FC Barcelona

FC Barcelona, often called Barça, is a renowned professional football club based in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain, famous for its attacking style of play, including the pioneering "tiki-taka" style emphasizing short passes and possession. Founded in 1899 by Joan Gamper and a diverse group of football enthusiasts, the club has a rich history and has achieved significant success, winning numerous national and international titles, including La Liga, Copa del Rey, and the UEFA Champions League.
